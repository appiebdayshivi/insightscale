Know About Us.

InsightScale is a platform designed to serve diverse purposes, primarily dedicated to empowering individuals, especially students and youth to pursue their passions as careers in the near future. Its core mission revolves around supporting individuals in pursuing their distinctive passions, interests, and unconventional ideas. The platform champions the belief that every person deserves encouragement and the freedom to live for their passions. InsightScale is committed to simplifying and enhancing access to opportunities for students, emphasizing the allocation of resources in a straightforward and accessible manner. At its essence, InsightScale operates on the philosophy that fostering ambitious dreams leads to a significantly enriched and fulfilling life experience.
"The better the dreams, the better life seems".
The quote stated above encapsulates InsightScale's ethos.

Our Incentives

InsightScale follows it's 3 main incentives known as the three E's :

1. Emergency to Connect :

One of our major concern is to connect students of similar or contrasting interests, ideas and experiences together on a social channel. We also provide students with occasional projects and meetings to help them interact with each other and exchange idea and interests.



2. Employment :

The will amongst the students to get employed based on their skill set and to earn money to manage their expenses and understand the value of managing their finances is what we highly appreciate and encourage. Ergo, we employ students to work for us and complete the assigned tasks to help them earn money, to help them manage their expenses and to help them attain finance managing skills.



3. Effective Research Clubs :

Research is undoubtedly the highest rewarding activity. Whether it be from researching over choosing the right career option or to seek for opportunities to even building a business. Therefore, valuing the research factor we provide Research Clubs to help students facilitate their area of knowledge and grow better.
